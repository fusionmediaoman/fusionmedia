Founded in 2010, Fusion Media is an independent multimedia production studio based in Muscat, Sultanate of Oman. We specialize in video production — from scripting, cinematography, and lighting, to editing, color grading, and visual effects. 

Our studio also offers professional event and commercial photography, along with advanced retouching and color correction. Whether it’s aerial tours, real estate, or special events, we deliver high-quality visual content using advanced 4K drone systems and creative precision. 

In audio, we provide full services including background music, jingles, voice-overs (Arabic, English, Hindi), and complete recording, mixing, and mastering for artists and individuals. 

We’re proud to be working with OQ, one of Oman’s leading energy companies, supporting their media needs with focused, reliable production services.
